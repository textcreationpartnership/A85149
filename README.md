1. A VINDICATION OF THE SCRIPTURES, In Anſwer to the Scottiſh Contradictors.

    _ To that which thou calls touching the Scriptures themſelves.

    _ Concerning the Light, and Chriſt the way to the Father.

    _ Concerning the Law.

    _ Concerning Sinne.

    _ Concerning Juſtification, and the end of the Righteous and wicked, or unrighteous.

    _ Concerning Repentance.

    _ Concerning Reading, Singings and Prayer, Baptiſme and the Lords Suffer &c.

    _ Concerning Elders, Miniſters Maintenance, Immediate calling, and Immediate teaching, &c.

    _ Concerning Queſtions.

    _ To that of thine concerning Civill honour, &c.

    _ Concerning Oaths.

    _ Selfe Contradictions.

    _ Concerning the new Birth, and the word in the heart.

    _ This is a Teſtimony againſt that Pamphlet (nameleſſe Author) called, The ſerious reveiw of ſome Principles of the Quakers.
